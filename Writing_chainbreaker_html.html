Chapter 1 — "Chains of Birth"
A massive city sprawling beneath an iron-colored sky. Floating platforms circle a colossal Chains-Tower above the slums.
NEXUS CITY — LOWER CHAIN DISTRICT
Young Modiri, 16, stands in the alley. Chains wrap his wrists. His eyes glow faint green.
"These chains... they burn."
KRAKK!
The chains crack. Green energy erupts from his hands in a burst of light.
A CHAIN ENFORCER looms above — twice Modiri's size, armored in void-black with purple glowing visors.
Modiri digs his feet into the asphalt. Cracks spider out beneath him.
"I won't be bound by your system anymore."
PULSE!
Massive energy explosion. The chain enforcer flies backwards. Green chain-energy wraps Modiri's arms like living armor.
ELEMENTIS UNLOCKED

Universe Guide

World & Lore

01

⚡ Power Systems

🌿
ELEMENTIS
The manipulation of natural energy drawn from the earth's core and channeled through the user's bio-chain. The oldest and most versatile power system — fire, water, wind, earth, and void variants exist.
Tier
🧠
NEURO
The weaponization of cognitive energy. Neuro users can shatter minds, project illusions, rewrite short-term memory, and at extreme mastery — predict an opponent's next 3 moves in real time.
Tier
🔥
CORE
Raw physical energy amplified from the body's biological core reactor. The most straightforward but dangerous system — Core overload can vaporize the user. Kira Solstice is the youngest Core-Class A in history.
Tier
⛓
CHAIN FORCE
A legendary variant of Elementis unique to Modiri. Chain Force lets the user create, shape, and detonate energy chains at will — the chains can bind, slice, amplify, or suppress other powers.
Tier
02

🔴 Kyōken Shifts

⚠ KYŌKEN — THE VIOLENT RESONANCE
Kyōken (凶犬 — "Violent Hound") is a state of total bio-chain resonance where a wielder's power system reaches critical sync with their emotional core. It amplifies all abilities by a multiplier tied to the depth of the emotional trigger — rage, grief, love, or will. However, each Kyōken stage brings the user closer to Bio-Chain Collapse — total cellular failure. Only three fighters in ChainBreaker history have survived Stage IV.
I
PULSE
Eyes glow. Aura visible. Power ×2. Sustainable for hours.
II
SURGE
Veins illuminate. Physical limit broken. Power ×5. 30min max.
III
FRACTURE
Bones reshape. Energy corona. Power ×12. Body begins to crack.
IV
OBLIVION
Form destabilizes. Dimensional rift opens. Power ×∞. Survival: 3%

🌍 World Regions

⛓ NEXUS CITY
The central megacity. Home to the Chain Council and the Towers. Where it all begins.
🌊 TIDEMARK
Coastal city-state. Elementis water users dominate. Zhen Vargos' homeland.
🔥 THE IGNITION
Volcanic region. Core users emerge here. Extreme temperatures forge stronger wielders.
🧠 GREY SPIRE
The floating academic citadel. Neuro research hub. Where Seraph was trained.
🌑 THE VOID FIELDS
Unmapped territory beyond the Chain Wall. Void elementis users originate here. Forbidden.
⭕ LOWER CHAINS
The slums beneath Nexus City. Modiri's origin. 78% of the population lives here.
